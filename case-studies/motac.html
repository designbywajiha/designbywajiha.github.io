Project Overview

The Malaysia Truly Asia app serves as the official digital platform for Malaysia's Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture (MOTAC). The goal was to create an engaging, intuitive experience that helps international and domestic travelers discover attractions, cultural events, and essential services across Malaysia.

Working with the PayNest FinTech team, I led the UX/UI design for both the consumer-facing mobile app and the administrative CMS used by tourism officials to manage content, events, and service listings.
