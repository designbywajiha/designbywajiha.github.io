Project Overview

UniVision is an education ecosystem platform that democratizes access to university information for students worldwide. The platform aggregates data from over 2,500 universities, making it easier for students to research, compare, and ultimately choose the right institution for their educational journey.

Working remotely with teams across Dubai, Malaysia, and Pakistan, I led the UX/UI design for both mobile and web platforms, focusing on creating intuitive experiences that help students navigate the overwhelming world of higher education choices.
