Project Overview

Dubai Smart Travel (DST) is a groundbreaking fintech platform developed for the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA). The system enables travelers to digitally purchase entry and exit vouchers for UAE land and sea border crossings, eliminating cash handling and reducing processing times at immigration counters.

As the lead UX/UI designer at PayNest FinTech, I was responsible for translating complex regulatory, security, and payment requirements into intuitive user flows that millions of travelers could confidently navigate.
